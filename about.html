About Us

Let Our Voices Be Heard

We at Freakonomics Podcast, strive to have people's voices heard. Those who are struggling to fit in the mold, specifically, Teenagers struggling with anxiety, is to be our main topic. We are aiming to let other teenagers who are struggling with anxiety have a peace in mind that they are not alone. With our podcast, we want to let people share their experiences with Anxiety, how they live with it, and some tips that they may want to share.
